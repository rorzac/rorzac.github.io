FAQ

How to sell cryptocurrency?

In order to sell cryptocurrency, you need to do the following steps on the main page website exmocoin.net.
1. Select the cryptocurrency you want to sell in the "Give" column.
2. Select "I receive" in the column. Where do you want to get money.
3. Specify the amount you want to exchange or receive.
4. Fill in your data in the "Data Entry" column: e-mail, phone number to which it is attached wallet or card number.
5. Press the "Exchange" button.
6. In the pop-up window you will see our details for the transfer. Copy these details and send them what you need amount of cryptocurrency.
7. After creating the application, you will receive a letter in the mail. You can track the status of your application in a popup window.

How long does it take to process an application?

The exchange is carried out automatically and takes an average of 15 to 30 minutes after the payment is received on the application. For some areas, the processing of the application may take from 3 to 6 hours. If you transfer cryptocurrencies to us, please make sure you set up enough network fees for fast processing.

How can I track the status of my application?

You can see the status of the application in the pop-up window that appears after clicking the "Exchange" button. First, our details are shown there, according to which you must make a payment. As soon as our system finds your payment, the status will be updated in real time. Once the transfer is completed, the status will update to Completed.

Why is my application taking so long to process?

If you do not see a notification in the application status that your payment has been received, check if the funds were debited during the transfer. Perhaps you did not take into account the commission of the payment system or the bank and you did not have enough funds for the transfer. Or, for some technical reason, the payment system or the bank did not miss your transfer. If payment for a claim is not received within 30 minutes of its creation, we will cancel the claim and you will have to create a new claim.

I transferred the amount less than required. What to do?

Your exchange will not be processed and completed automatically as we expect the exact amount. You need to contact the service support, we will offer possible options and process your exchange manually. Commissions of banks, payment systems, cryptocurrency networks and the service itself are your responsibility.

I translated more than required. What to do?

Your exchange will not be processed and completed automatically as we expect the exact amount. You need to contact the service support, we will offer possible options and process your exchange manually. Commissions of banks, payment systems, cryptocurrency networks and the service itself are your responsibility.

Can I refuse the exchange and return the funds if I have already paid for the application?

If your application has not yet been completed and you see the "Cancel" button, click it and contact technical support for help. Commissions of banks, payment systems, cryptocurrency networks and the service itself are your responsibility. If the application is completed, then the return is no longer possible.

How often does the exchange rate change and why?

Cryptocurrency rates on coincat.in change in real time without reloading the page and depend on many factors: the rate of cryptocurrencies on international exchanges, the number of reserves on our service, supply and demand for cryptocurrencies. The rate is fixed at the time of registration of the application for the exchange.

What is the mode of operation of your site? What time can I make an exchange?

All transactions are carried out automatically 24/7.

Will I need to verify the card?

Card verification is not required to sell cryptocurrency. To buy cryptocurrency with bank cards, card verification is required.
