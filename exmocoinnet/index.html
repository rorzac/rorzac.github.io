Why do you need card verification?

Card verification in order to prevent unauthorized card withdrawals. Recently, cases of hacking accounts of Internet banks have become more frequent, unfortunately it is not difficult for attackers to make a duplicate SIM card and transfer funds. In this regard, many exchange services, in order not to be involved in such fraudulent schemes, have introduced a card verification procedure.

Why do you need confirmations in the bitcoin system?

When receiving bitcoin coins, the recipient cannot immediately use them. Once a transfer is made, it is sent to the Bitcoin network for execution and must be included in a block to become confirmed. The process of including a transfer in the found block is called transaction confirmation. Inclusion in 1 block = 1 confirmation, when there are 2 or more such confirmations, the transfer is considered confirmed, and onemoment.cc operators, in turn, process the request. This method was introduced to protect against reuse of the same bitcoins.
